Our hospital operations management service for community hospitals provides solutions for small hospital and health facilities in North America.


At Franklin Healthcare Management we know that small hospitals and health facilities are integral to local communities, with many of these services being the only available health care in their areas. Local health services are also a major source of employment in rural and remote areas, making them a valuable community hub. However, with 19 small hospitals closing in the past 12 months and 67% of US hospitals losing money, these facilities need help to stay afloat.
